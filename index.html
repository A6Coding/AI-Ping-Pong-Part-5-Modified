AI PING PONG







Created By: Aryan Dhoot

Instructions

1. First keep your laptop screen straight

2. Move yourself around 3 - 4 feet from the laptop

3. Move your right wrist up and down. A red dot should apear

4. Now press the play button and again distance yourself about 3 - 4 feet from the computer

5. The red paddle is yours. Move your right wrist up and down to move the paddle
